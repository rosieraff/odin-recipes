Caramelized Pork Bowls

Want a melt-in-your mouth easy-to-make dinner? Look no further.

This simple recipe using just a few ingredients is guaranteed to make everyone ask for seconds. Any ground meat will do well in this recipe, I just prefer to use ground pork. The best thing about this recipe is how versatile it is. You are able to add or omit any ingredients you want to customize to your preferences.

Ingredients

    Necessary ingredients
  • 1 pound of ground pork (or any ground meat)
  • 5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • Optional ingredients
  • Cooking oil of choice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 green onion diced, green and white parts kept separate
  • Chiles or peppers: thai, habanero, jalapeno etc
  • Chicken bouillon
    • Optional toppings
  • Tomato, diced
  • Cucumber, diced

Steps

Prep
  1. Dice/mince onion, garlic, ginger, white parts of green onion, and any chiles/peppers
  2. Green parts of green onions diced and set aside
  3. Combine brown sugar, soy sauce, and bouillon and set aside
    4. Cooking
  4. Heat pan over medium heat with a little bit of oil
  5. Cook onions until browned
  6. Add ginger, garlic, white parts of green onion, chiles/peppers
  7. Add ground pork and cook until pink is gone
  8. Add brown sugar, soy sauce, and chicken bouillon
  9. Turn heat to high and cook meat 2 minutes at a time until meat is caramelized to your liking. Note: May take 10+ minutes, so be patient until it all comes together
  10. Top with: green parts of green onion, cucumber, and tomatoes
