Turkey Power Bowl

This meal is loaded with good stuff to fuel your body and make your tastebuds sing

This turkey bowl is packed full of protein, fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants. What I love most about this meal is how customizable it is. Missing an ingredient? No worries! It'll taste great without it. Want to try a different vegetable? Go for it! Add and remove ingredients to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Frozen bell peppers and onions
  • Frozen corn
  • Can of black bean, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • Shredded cheese; I prefer to shred half a block of pepper jack and half a block of sharp cheddar
  • Cilantro chopped
  • Fresh lemon, cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp of each these seasonings in a bowl: garlic powder, salt, pepper, paprika, and tomato bouillon
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Heat ground turkey until half cooked
  2. Add in garlic cloves plus bowl of mixed seasonings. I like to add in a little water for moisture. Cook fully.
  3. Add in drained black beans, frozen peppers and onions, and the frozen corn
  4. Add red pepper flakes, mix everything together
  5. Add shredded cheese on top and cover for 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted
  6. Once everything is mixed together, add tomato and cilantro
